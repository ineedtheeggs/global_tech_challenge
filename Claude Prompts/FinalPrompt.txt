в”Ӯ # aFRR Opportunity Cost Calculator                                           в”Ӯ
в”Ӯ                                                                              в”Ӯ
в”Ӯ Quantifies opportunity costs for gas peaker plants (CCGT) participating in   в”Ӯ
в”Ӯ aFRR spin-up (balancing) markets in the DE-LU bidding zone. Uses OLS         в”Ӯ
в”Ӯ regression to model the relationship between Clean Spark Spread (CSS) and    в”Ӯ
в”Ӯ aFRR-up capacity prices across three market regime conditions (High / Medium в”Ӯ
в”Ӯ  / Low gas generation).                                                      в”Ӯ
в”Ӯ                                                                              в”Ӯ
в”Ӯ > The motivation and initial vision for this analysis is documented in       в”Ӯ
в”Ӯ [`Resources/Vision.odt`](Resources/Vision.odt).                              в”Ӯ
в”Ӯ                                                                              в”Ӯ
в”Ӯ ---                                                                          в”Ӯ
в”Ӯ                                                                              в”Ӯ
в”Ӯ ## Background                                                                в”Ӯ
в”Ӯ                                                                              в”Ӯ
в”Ӯ Gas prices are a key driver of opportunity costs for gas peakers bidding     в”Ӯ
в”Ӯ into aFRR markets. When CSS (a proxy for plant profitability) rises, plants  в”Ӯ
в”Ӯ demand higher compensation to withhold capacity for balancing. This          в”Ӯ
в”Ӯ sensitivity also depends on how many gas plants are online вҖ” a "regime"      в”Ӯ
в”Ӯ effect that this model captures via three fixed MW thresholds derived from   в”Ӯ
в”Ӯ K-Means clustering of historical CCGT generation data.                       в”Ӯ
в”Ӯ                                                                              в”Ӯ
в”Ӯ This project covers the period **2020вҖ“2026**, selected to isolate            в”Ӯ
в”Ӯ CCGT-dominated aFRR price formation (post-EU-ETS carbon pricing, pre-BESS    в”Ӯ
в”Ӯ boom).                                                                       в”Ӯ
в”Ӯ                                                                              в”Ӯ
в”Ӯ > Side scripts used for preprocessing (aFRR bid aggregation, regime centroid в”Ӯ
в”Ӯ  derivation via K-Means) are not included in this repository for brevity.    в”Ӯ
в”Ӯ Their outputs are committed as reference files in `data/references/`.        в”Ӯ

     # aFRR Opportunity Cost Calculator

     Quantifies opportunity costs for gas peaker plants (CCGT) participating
      in aFRR spin-up (balancing) markets in the DE-LU bidding zone. Uses
     OLS regression to model the relationship between Clean Spark Spread
     (CSS) and aFRR-up capacity prices across three market regime conditions
      (High / Medium / Low gas generation).

     > The motivation and initial vision for this analysis is documented in
     [`Resources/Vision.odt`](Resources/Vision.odt).
