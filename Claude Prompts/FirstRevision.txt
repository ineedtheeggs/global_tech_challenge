  1. DA price is already in CSS ‚ÄĒ CSS = DA_Price - (Gas_Price / ő∑) -

  coefficients: ő≤‚āĀ directly tells you "for ‚ā¨1/MWh increase in CSS, aFRR bid
  price rises by ő≤‚āĀ ‚ā¨/MW" ‚ÄĒ clean and actionable, no confounding.
